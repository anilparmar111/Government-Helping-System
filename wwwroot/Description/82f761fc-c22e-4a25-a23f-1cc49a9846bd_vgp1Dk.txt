<h2><span id="Challenge_yourself!"></span><span id="Challenge_yourself.21">Challenge yourself!</span></h2>
<p><a title="Categories" href="https://algorithmist.com/wiki/Categories">Classifications</a>&nbsp;- Problems and articles by categories.</p>
<p><a title="UVa Problemset" href="https://algorithmist.com/wiki/UVa_Problemset">UVa Problemset</a>&nbsp;-&nbsp;<a href="http://onlinejudge.org/" rel="nofollow">http://onlinejudge.org/</a>&nbsp;- The Valladolid University Online Judge. Over&nbsp;<strong>N</strong>&nbsp;problems, for a reasonable value of&nbsp;<strong>N</strong>. The problems are culled from old contests, and online contests.</p>
<p><a title="ACM-ICPC Live Archive" href="https://algorithmist.com/wiki/ACM-ICPC_Live_Archive">ACM-ICPC Live Archive</a>&nbsp;-&nbsp;<a href="http://livearchive.onlinejudge.org/" rel="nofollow">http://livearchive.onlinejudge.org/</a>&nbsp;- The 2000's ACM-ICPC Live Archive Around the World. Contains actual problems from regionals and finals from 2000 on.</p>
<p><a title="Topcoder" href="https://algorithmist.com/wiki/Topcoder">Topcoder</a>&nbsp;-&nbsp;<a href="http://www.topcoder.com/" rel="nofollow">http://www.topcoder.com</a>&nbsp;- Weekly programming competitions from algorithms to components to marathons.</p>
<p><a title="Project Euler" href="https://algorithmist.com/wiki/Project_Euler">Project Euler</a>&nbsp;-&nbsp;<a href="http://projecteuler.net/" rel="nofollow">http://projecteuler.net</a>&nbsp;- Project Euler consists challenging mathematical or computer science problems.</p>
<p><a title="Sphere Online Judge" href="https://algorithmist.com/wiki/Sphere_Online_Judge">Sphere Online Judge</a>&nbsp;-&nbsp;<a href="http://spoj.com/" rel="nofollow">http://spoj.com</a>&nbsp;- One of the earliest judges with many support for many different languages.</p>
